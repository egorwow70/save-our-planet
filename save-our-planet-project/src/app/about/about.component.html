Hello

It is Save Our Planet Project

Our project is based on planting trees around the world. You can select any country in the countries menu and select any tree in the trees menu. If you donate a tree to a country, you will receive honey points which you can convert in your profile to health insurance with which you can get a good discount in any medical institution. Our project is still young enough but we try to make the world a better place. Join our project and make the world green! Egor Samuilionok was engaged in this project, the development of which was spent about a month. All ideas were invented either in the shower or in the soft bed. Also inspiration was my imagination and rich inner world. Our office is located in the city of Mogilev at 10 Lane Oil House.
